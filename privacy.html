Privacy

For local development, your Gemini API key lives in your environment (or in the backend file if you pasted it). Reminders are stored in your Firebase project, under your control.

  • Chat messages are sent to Gemini via your backend for responses.
  • Reminder data is saved in Firestore under your project.
  • Anonymous Firebase Auth is used locally; you can switch to real auth later.

In production, use stricter Firestore rules and a proper auth provider, and never expose private keys in frontend code.
